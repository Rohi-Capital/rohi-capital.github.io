Company Overview

Who we are

Rohi Capital Limited is an investment and asset management company established to provide innovative, disciplined, and client-focused investment solutions to individuals, businesses, institutions, and sophisticated investors.

Rohi Capital is founded on the belief that wealth creation should be guided by integrity, prudent stewardship, and long-term thinking. We exist to help our clients navigate complex financial markets, preserve capital, unlock growth opportunities, and build lasting legacies.

Globally there are significant investment opportunities. However, these opportunities require sound judgment, disciplined execution, and trusted partnerships. Rohi Capital combines local market expertise with global investment principles to deliver investment solutions that align with our clients' aspirations and risk profiles.

We are building an institution designed not merely for today's opportunities, but for generations of sustainable value creation.

Our Vision

To be one of Africa's most trusted and respected investment management institutions, renowned for integrity, innovation, disciplined investing, and exceptional client outcomes.

To empower individuals and institutions to build, preserve, and transfer wealth through professional investment management, sound governance, and enduring partnerships.
